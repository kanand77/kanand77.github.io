What the words Engineer, Athlete, and Leader mean to me.

Engineer

Lifelong Learning. CS as a field is growing at rapid pace, with new frameworks and technology seemingly being released every week. To stay at the cutting edge, I embrace the growth mindset and dedicate myself to experiment with new tools and tech stacks, never shying away from an opportunity to step outside my comfort zone.

Athlete

Perseverence & Teamwork. I am a competitve tennis player, having competed in state and national level tournaments since 8 years old and representing my high school and Stanford at the highest levels. My tennis journey has taught me the power of persisting when all hope seems lost, communicating and finding strength within your teammates and coaches, and finally facing any situation with hope.

Leader

Risk & Initiative. Leadership builds beyond the first two principles, instilling a sense of urgency and excitability each endeavor. Leadership is advocacy, standing up for novel ideas, taking responsibility and ownership for new initiatives, and uniting and motivating teams towards a common goal. I hold myself to this standard and challenge myself to consistently work towards these ideals to better myself and my community.
