Practice Coordinator

  • Skills: Tennis
  • Organization: Stanford Club Tennis
  • Duration: September 2019 to Present

Compete in tournaments, travel with the team and organize practice for Stanford Club Tennis

Tennis has played a huge role in my life ever since I first picked up the racquet when I was 4. Competing for my high school, traveling around the country for national tournaments, winning titles with my teammates, tennis has taught me countless valuable lessons in perseverance and teammwork. I wanted to continue building these skills and enjoy the game in college which naturally attracted me to the club tennis team.

My freshman year I played many close matches, representing Stanford at tournaments far and near. I found great community with the team and discovered many of my closest friends at Stanford here. I wanted to give back the following year and ensure that practices were just as fantastic as they were for me. I was elected practic coordinator and alongside my partner we strived to make practice sessions organized, directed, and fun.

Kavin Anand
