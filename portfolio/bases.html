Selected into Frosh Battalion and elected Director of Growth, I was immersed in the entrepreneurial spirit

Stanford BASES is the premier entrepenurship club at Stanford, with many events catered towards building and funding startups like the Startup Career Fair to bringing in influential keynote speakers who've gone through the startup journey. My freshman year, I was selected as one of 25 students from a pool of over 300 to be a member of Frosh Battalion based on my leadership principles. In this group, we had exclusive access to company treks where we'd meet with many innovative firms in the bay and mingle with high level personnel and had plenty of workshops to build up our resume, public speaking skills and online portfolios.

The following year, I was elected as Director of Growth in BASES and focused on organizing Frosh Battalion for the next batch of students as well as prepare the plethora of events that made my freshman year experience so wonderful.

Kavin Anand
